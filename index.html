About Myself

I am an Embedded Engineer with a strong foundation in Research & Development (R&D) and hands-on expertise in Robotics and Intelligent Embedded Systems. My primary focus is on designing reliable, real-time, microcontroller-based solutions for industrial and safety-critical applications.
